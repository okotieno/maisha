About Us

We are a team of dedicated healthcare professionals who are passionate about increasing access to oral health and general health prevention information. We have designed a platform where anyone can learn about a wide range of health topics in an easy to understand format. The topics are covered in numerous languages.

We are also organizing campaigns in schools, hospitals and workplaces to help educate the next generation on important prevention advice. So far, we have carried out workshops in Kenya and Mali. We will continue to build on these projects and partnerships
